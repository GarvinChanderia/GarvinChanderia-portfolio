HR Offboarding Automation.

Standardised enterprise offboarding through automated checklists, IT access revocation tracking, and auto-generated summary presentations.

Context

Offboarding is one of those processes every company has and nobody owns. The result is inconsistent execution, missed IT revocations, and zero institutional memory of what knowledge walked out the door. This project systematised the process into a repeatable, automated workflow.

What Was Built

  • Dynamic Checklist Generator: Role-aware exit checklists factoring department, access level, and system permissions.
  • IT Revocation Tracker: Systematic deactivation tracking across email, VPN, SaaS tools, and building access.
  • Knowledge Transfer Templates: Structured documents capturing critical information from departing team members.
  • Summary Deck Generator: Automated PowerPoint creation summarising each offboarding case for HR records.

Role: Product ops · Automation builder

Stack: Python, PowerPoint automation, workflow scripting

Status: Shipped
