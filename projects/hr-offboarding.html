HR Offboarding Automation.

Streamlining enterprise offboarding with automated workflows and presentation generation.

Overview

An internal operations automation project focused on standardising the HR offboarding process. Built automated workflows for exit checklist generation, IT access revocation tracking, knowledge transfer documentation, and manager notification sequences. Included automated presentation generation for offboarding summaries.

What Was Built

  • Exit Checklist Generator: Automated checklist creation based on role, department, and access level.
  • IT Revocation Tracker: Systematic tracking of account deactivations across systems.
  • Knowledge Transfer Docs: Templated documentation for outgoing employees to capture critical knowledge.
  • Offboarding Summary Deck: Auto-generated presentations summarising each offboarding case.
