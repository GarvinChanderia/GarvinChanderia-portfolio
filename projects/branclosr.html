BranClosr.

A dual-sided marketplace that compresses influencer marketing deal cycles from 30 days to under 48 hours. Full-stack platform targeting D2C brands and mid-tier creators in India.

Problem

Influencer deals stall in email chains, vague pricing, and contract ambiguity. Brands lose campaign momentum. Creators lose income to friction. The industry standard of 2–4 weeks per deal is structurally broken — it's a coordination problem, not a complexity problem.

Solution

BranClosr standardises terms, structures negotiation into real-time deal rooms, and automates contract generation. The platform handles the full arc — discovery, negotiation, agreement, deliverable tracking, payment — in a single environment designed for speed.

Technical Architecture

56 TypeScript modules across a Next.js 16 App Router codebase. Supabase backend with PostgreSQL 15+, row-level security, and real-time subscriptions. State managed through Zustand, TanStack React Query, and URL search params. Forms validated with React Hook Form + Zod. Auth via Supabase SSR (email/password + Google OAuth). Styled with Tailwind CSS and shadcn/ui patterns. Tested with Vitest + React Testing Library.

What's Shipped

  • SEO-optimised public landing page with OpenGraph and JSON-LD
  • Role-aware dashboard (brand and creator views)
  • Campaign management with creation, filtering, and detail views
  • Deal rooms with timeline, status badges, and negotiation flows
  • Deliverables tracking grouped by campaign
  • Notification system with mark-as-read and grouping
  • Full auth flow with protected routes and role guards
