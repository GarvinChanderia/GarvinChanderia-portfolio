B2C SaaS

BranClosr.

Brand–influencer deal acceleration. 30 days compressed to under 48 hours.

The Problem

Influencer marketing deals take 2–4 weeks to close. Brands and creators lose momentum in endless email chains, negotiation back-and-forth, and contract ambiguity. BranClosr compresses the entire cycle — discovery, negotiation, contract, payment — into a single platform targeting sub-48-hour campaign cycles.

Architecture

Full-stack PWA built with Next.js App Router, TypeScript strict mode, and Supabase/PostgreSQL with row-level security. The platform features real-time deal rooms, AI-assisted contract generation, and integrated payment flows. Designed mobile-first for the Indian creator economy.

Key Features

  • Deal Rooms: Real-time negotiation spaces with structured offer/counter-offer flows.
  • Smart Matching: AI-driven brand–influencer pairing based on niche, engagement, and budget fit.
  • Contract Engine: Auto-generated agreements with configurable terms and e-signatures.
  • Creator Profiles: Verified metrics, portfolio showcases, and rate cards.

Dev Stack

Full documentation suite generated — PRD, Tech Architecture, Design Document, AI Implementation Contract, and AI Rules. Seven governance documents total, all stored in the Obsidian vault with proper frontmatter and backlinks.
