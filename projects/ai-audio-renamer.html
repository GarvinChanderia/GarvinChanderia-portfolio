AI Audio Renamer.

Semantic file organisation for music producers, powered by AI.

The Problem

Music producers accumulate thousands of audio files with cryptic names — "final_v3_REAL.wav", "beat_new_new.mp3". Finding the right sample during a session kills creative flow. The AI Audio Renamer analyses audio content and renames files with meaningful, searchable labels.

How It Works

The tool uses OpenAI Whisper for audio analysis and GPT for semantic labelling. It processes batches of audio files, identifies instrument type, mood, tempo, and key, then renames them with a consistent taxonomy that makes any sample library instantly browsable.

Features

  • Batch Processing: Drop a folder, get it renamed in minutes.
  • Local-First: Supports Ollama for fully offline processing.
  • Smart Taxonomy: Genre → Instrument → Mood → BPM → Key naming convention.

Role: Solo Builder

Stack: Python, OpenAI Whisper, GPT-4, Ollama
