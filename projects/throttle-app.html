ThrottleApp.

Safety, connectivity, and the joy of riding — in one ecosystem.

Overview

ThrottleApp is a mobile app built as a comprehensive companion for motorcyclists. It addresses the fragmentation in rider-tech — navigation, social connectivity, safety, and trip documentation — through a single, performance-optimised interface. 300+ downloads, 200+ user interviews conducted.

Key Features

  • Crash Detection: Accelerometer + gyroscope data to detect incidents and alert emergency contacts.
  • Live Location Sharing: Real-time tracking for group rides — no rider left behind.
  • Automated Trip Logging: Route data, speed profiles, and photos without manual input.
  • Quick Dial: One-tap emergency calling from the ride screen.

Mission

"To empower motorbike enthusiasts with innovative technology, enhancing safety, connectivity, and the joy of riding." The project focused on identifying core rider pain points — safety during solo rides, difficulty managing group dynamics — and solving them through an MVP that prioritises performance and battery efficiency.
