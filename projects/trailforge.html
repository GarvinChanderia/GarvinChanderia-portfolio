Community & Brand

TrailForge Club.

A motorcycle riding community built from scratch — brand, culture, and all.

The Concept

TrailForge is a motorcycle riding community with a complete brand identity system. Unlike generic riding groups, TrailForge was designed as a brand-first community — with structured onboarding, physical merchandise, and a culture playbook that makes every new member feel like they belong to something built to last.

What Was Built

  • Full Brand Identity: Logo system, typography, colour palette, and brand guidelines.
  • Starter Kits: Physical welcome packages for new members — stickers, lanyards, and printed materials.
  • Merchandise: Custom-designed stickers, lanyards, and riding accessories.
  • Onboarding System: Structured member welcome flow with role assignments and ride-level tiers.

Design Philosophy

Every touchpoint was designed to feel premium but accessible. The visual language draws from adventure and exploration — muted earth tones, bold sans-serif typography, and iconography inspired by trail markers and topographic maps. The brand should feel like it belongs on a riding jacket, not just a screen.

Role: Founder / Brand Designer

Tools: Figma, Canva, Print Production
