Brand · Community

TrailForge Club.

A motorcycle riding community designed brand-first — with a complete identity system, physical merchandise, and a structured onboarding experience that treats community as a product.

Philosophy

Most riding groups are informal WhatsApp clusters with no identity or structure. TrailForge was built differently — as a brand that happens to be a community, not the other way around. Every touchpoint is designed: the visual language, the physical materials, the member journey from first ride to regular status.

What Was Built

  • Brand Identity System: Logo, typography, colour palette, and comprehensive usage guidelines.
  • Starter Kit: Physical welcome package — printed booklet, branded stickers (rectangular and square formats), emergency contact sticker for gear, and a branded lanyard.
  • Merchandise Line: Custom stickers, meme packs, and 100kmph-branded lanyards designed for production at scale.
  • Onboarding Flow: Structured member welcome process with role assignment and ride-level progression tiers.

Design Language

Muted earth tones, bold sans-serif type, and iconography drawn from trail markers and topographic maps. The visual system is designed to feel equally at home on a riding jacket, a screen, and a printed kit — because the brand lives across all three.
