Product Strategy

Smartwatch Landscape.

A structured market analysis of the wearable technology sector — mapping competitive positioning, feature evolution trajectories, and whitespace opportunities for new entrants.

Scope

The study covers the full smartwatch market across premium, mid-range, fitness-first, and fashion-first segments. Analysis includes Apple Watch, Samsung Galaxy Watch, Garmin, Fitbit, and emerging players in the Indian market. Each brand is evaluated on health sensor capability, GPS/cellular features, battery architecture, and software ecosystem maturity.

Deliverables

  • Market Segmentation Matrix: Four-quadrant positioning map with price-point overlay.
  • Feature Evolution Timeline: Generational capability tracking across health, connectivity, and battery.
  • Competitive Feature Matrix: Brand-by-brand comparison across 20+ feature dimensions.
  • Opportunity Analysis: Identified gaps in the Indian mid-range segment and niche-specific verticals.

Role: Product researcher & analyst

Deliverable: Research report (PDF)
