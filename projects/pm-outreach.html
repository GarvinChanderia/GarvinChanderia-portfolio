PM Outreach Engine.

An automated professional networking system applying the same infrastructure principles as the Dental Revenue Engine to strategic job search — lead discovery, personalised messaging, and multi-channel delivery.

Approach

Treated the job search as a product problem: define the target segment, build a pipeline, measure conversion, and iterate on messaging. The system compiles verified HR and PM leads across target companies, generates personalised outreach per company context, and delivers via automated sequences with reply monitoring.

Target Segments

  • Big 4 Advisory: PwC AC India (referral-backed), Deloitte USI, EY GDS, KPMG India
  • Pune Product Companies: ElasticRun, Icertis, Mindtickle, Avalara
  • AI/ML-focused roles: AI consulting, product management, hybrid/remote options

Current State

The Gmail sender account was blocked after high-volume outreach. Pivoting primary delivery to WhatsApp Business API (Meta Cloud API) while maintaining the lead pipeline and personalisation layer. The underlying automation infrastructure — n8n workflows, lead databases, template engines — remains fully operational.

Role: Sole builder

Stack: n8n, Python, Claude MCP, Gmail / WhatsApp Business API

Status: Active — channel migration in progress
