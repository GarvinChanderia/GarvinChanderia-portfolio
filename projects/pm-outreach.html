PM Outreach Engine.

Automated job search system targeting AI consulting and PM roles at 18–25 LPA.

The System

An automated outreach pipeline for PM and AI consulting roles — lead discovery, personalised emails, LinkedIn messaging, and batch sends. Built on the same n8n + Claude MCP stack that powers the Dental Revenue Engine, reapplied to job search with different lead sources and personalisation strategies.

Target Companies

  • Big 4: PwC AC India Advisory (referral contact), Deloitte USI, EY GDS, KPMG
  • West Pune Product Cos: ElasticRun, Icertis, Mindtickle, Avalara
  • Product-based: Companies offering hybrid/remote across Pune and Bengaluru

Pipeline

  1. Resume Scan → Role Mapping — Automated matching of skills to open roles.
  2. Lead List Compilation — HR and PM contacts verified and compiled into CSV.
  3. Cold Email + LinkedIn Templates — Personalised per company and role.
  4. Batch Send — Automated delivery with reply tracking.

Current Status

Gmail sender was blocked due to high-volume cold outreach — currently pivoting to WhatsApp Business API (Meta Cloud API) as the primary replacement channel. Lead list refresh underway for Big 4 targets.
